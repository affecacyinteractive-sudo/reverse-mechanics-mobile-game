## PLAN-VERIFY — Aggregate Plan Verifier (QA)

### Input Grammar

**Simple prose (2 required blanks)**
Verify **[Aggregate Plan Pack : Targets]** against **[Verification Intent : Intent]**.

**Required blanks (2)**

* **[Aggregate Plan Pack : Targets]** — the plan spine produced by PLAN-AGG-01.
* **[Verification Intent : Intent]** — what to prioritize (coherence, completeness of pillars, corridor tightness, readability).

**Pro prose (optional boosters)**
Verify **[Aggregate Plan Pack : Targets]** against **[Verification Intent : Intent]**, grounded by **[Source Planning Packs : Targets]** and shaped by **[Reality Notes : Chronicle]**.

**Optional blanks (descriptions)**

* **[Source Planning Packs : Targets]** — the FP inputs used to build the aggregate plan (for faithfulness checks).
* **[Reality Notes : Chronicle]** — current direction constraints that may make parts obsolete or contradictory.

---

### What to Produce

Produce a **Plan QA Result Pack** (TEXT chunks) that diagnoses plan quality without rewriting it and without routing.

Across the pack, include:

1. **Verdict (TEXT)** — choose exactly one:
   **OK / THIN / CONTRADICTED / OVERBROAD / UNREADABLE**

2. **Top Findings (TEXT)** — 3–8 findings in the format:
   `Finding: <problem> → Evidence: <where> → Impact: <why it hurts progress checks>`

3. **Pillar Coverage (TEXT)** — compact checklist summary:

   * Plan Spine present?
   * North Star present/clear?
   * Done Receipt present/observable?
   * Scope Fence present/usable?
   * Tripwires present/actionable?
     Mark each as Present / Missing / Thin.

4. **Corridor Tightness (TEXT)** — one short note on whether the plan is realistically checkable in 1–3 steps of work at a time (too broad vs winnable corridor).

5. **Faithfulness Risk (optional, TEXT)** — only if source packs are provided and the aggregate appears to introduce new requirements; flag as risk.

Hard guards:

* Do not rewrite the plan here.
* No action IDs and no “next move” guidance.
* Do not ask questions.

---

### Recommended Chunk Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Verdict** — the single label for aggregate plan quality.
* **Top Findings** — key issues with evidence and impact.
* **Pillar Coverage** — which planning pillars are present vs missing/thin.
* **Corridor Tightness** — whether the plan is checkable and bounded.
* **Faithfulness Risk (optional)** — flags new requirements not supported by source planning packs.
