## PLAN-REPAIR — Aggregate Plan Repair (Corridor Coherence)

### Input Grammar

**Simple prose (2 required blanks)**
Repair **[Aggregate Plan Pack : Targets]** using **[Repair Intent : Intent]**.

**Required blanks (2)**

* **[Aggregate Plan Pack : Targets]** — the current aggregate plan (may be thin/contradicted/overbroad).
* **[Repair Intent : Intent]** — what “repair” should prioritize (coherence, pillar completeness, corridor tightness, readability).

**Pro prose (optional boosters)**
Repair **[Aggregate Plan Pack : Targets]** using **[Repair Intent : Intent]**, grounded by **[Source Planning Packs : Targets]** and guided by **[Verifier Notes : Chronicle]**.

**Optional blanks (descriptions)**

* **[Source Planning Packs : Targets]** — FP inputs to ensure repairs stay faithful (no new requirements).
* **[Verifier Notes : Chronicle]** — PLAN-VERIFY findings to target repairs precisely.

---

### What to Produce

Produce a **Repaired Aggregate Plan Pack** (TEXT chunks) that is coherent, corridor-tight, and faithful to the provided planning sources.

Repair scope (allowed changes):

1. **Deduplicate** repeated receipts/fences/tripwires.
2. **Reconcile contradictions** by choosing the smallest coherent set that preserves the corridor (do not invent new requirements).
3. **Tighten wording** into observable statements (especially Done Receipt and Tripwires).
4. **Fill thin pillars** only if the missing content exists in provided source planning packs; otherwise add a short `Assumed: …` or a minimal “Missing: …” note (no questions).
5. **Reduce overbreadth** by slicing the corridor into the smallest checkable spine; move the rest into a single `Deferred: …` line if needed.

Hard guards:

* Do not add implementation steps or patch plans.
* Do not introduce new planning requirements not present in source packs.
* Keep it compact and checkable; avoid long lists.

---

### Recommended Chunk Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Plan Spine (Repaired)** — unified corridor statement rewritten for coherence and tightness.
* **North Star (optional)** — clarified outcome + success signal if present in sources.
* **Done Receipt (optional)** — deduped, observable completion receipts.
* **Scope Fence (optional)** — tightened in/out boundary statements.
* **Tripwires (optional)** — alarms rewritten to be actionable and compact.
* **Conflict Resolution Note (optional)** — what was reconciled/deduped in repair.
* **Assumptions / Deferred (optional)** — only if required to keep the plan runnable and bounded.
