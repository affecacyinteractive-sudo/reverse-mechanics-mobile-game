## PLAN-AGG-01 — Aggregate Plan Builder (FP Spine)

### Input Grammar

**Simple prose (2 required blanks)**
Aggregate **[Planning Packs : Targets]** into **[Aggregate Plan Intent : Intent]**.

**Required blanks (2)**

* **[Planning Packs : Targets]** — a bundle of planning outputs (FP keystones/packs such as north star, done receipt, scope fence, tripwires).
* **[Aggregate Plan Intent : Intent]** — why you want an aggregate plan (one corridor spine, compare progress later, avoid drift).

**Pro prose (optional boosters)**
Aggregate **[Planning Packs : Targets]** into **[Aggregate Plan Intent : Intent]**, constrained by **[Scope Preference : Intent]** and grounded by **[Reality Notes : Chronicle]**.

**Optional blanks (descriptions)**

* **[Scope Preference : Intent]** — whether to include only the latest corridor decisions or merge across a broader window.
* **[Reality Notes : Chronicle]** — any context about current direction (“we changed priorities”, “new constraints emerged”).

---

### What to Produce

Produce an **Aggregate Plan Pack** (TEXT chunks) that combines FP artifacts into one coherent corridor spine, without inventing new planning requirements.

Across the pack, include:

1. **Plan Spine (TEXT)** — 3–6 lines that unify the corridor: what we’re aiming for, how we know it’s done, what we refuse, and what alarms us early.
2. **North Star (optional, TEXT)** — if present in inputs, restate the active outcome + success signal in 1–3 lines.
3. **Done Receipt (optional, TEXT)** — if present, list 3–8 observable receipts that define “done” (keep concise).
4. **Scope Fence (optional, TEXT)** — if present, include:

   * in-scope core (2–4 lines)
   * out-of-scope exclusions (3–6 lines)
5. **Tripwires (optional, TEXT)** — if present, list 3–8 alarms with a one-line response posture each.
6. **Conflict Note (optional, TEXT)** — only if needed: 2–6 lines naming contradictions/duplicates detected across inputs, without resolving by inventing new goals.
7. **Assumptions (optional, TEXT)** — only if required; one-liners as **“Assumed: …”**.

Hard guards:

* Do not add implementation steps or patch plans.
* Do not introduce new constraints not present in planning packs.
* Keep it corridor-like: winnable, bounded, readable.

---

### Recommended Chunk Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Plan Spine** — the unified corridor statement that ties planning artifacts together.
* **North Star (optional)** — the active target outcome and success signal.
* **Done Receipt (optional)** — observable receipts that define completion.
* **Scope Fence (optional)** — what’s in vs explicitly out of scope.
* **Tripwires (optional)** — early alarms and response posture.
* **Conflict Note (optional)** — duplicates/contradictions detected across planning inputs.
* **Assumptions (optional)** — only necessary assumptions stated firmly.
