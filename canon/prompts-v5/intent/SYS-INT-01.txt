## SYS-INT-01 — Intent Capture (Auto-Wrap)

### Input Grammar

Wrap **[Raw User Message : RawText]** into **[Base Intent Chunk : Intent]**.

*(No blanks; this is automatic.)*

### What to Produce

Produce exactly **one** TEXT chunk in `Intent[]`:

* **Title:** `User Ask`
* **Body:** the raw user message, unchanged (except trimming leading/trailing whitespace)
* **Kind:** TEXT

Hard guard:

* Do not summarize.
* Do not rephrase.
* Do not add assumptions.

### Recommended Chunk/Seal Beat Categories

* **Raw Ask Wrapper** — a faithful container for the original message so downstream intent actions have a stable anchor.
