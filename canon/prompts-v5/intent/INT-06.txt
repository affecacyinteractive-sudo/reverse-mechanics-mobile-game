## INT-06 — Intent Scope Cutter (Make it Winnable)

### Input Grammar

**Simple prose (2 required blanks)**
Cut **[Intent Pack : Intent]** down to a winnable slice for **[Intended Use : Intent]**.

**Required blanks (2)**

* **[Intent Pack : Intent]** — the current intent chunks (often overbroad).
* **[Intended Use : Intent]** — what this intent will drive next.

**Pro prose (optional boosters)**
Cut **[Intent Pack : Intent]** down to a winnable slice for **[Intended Use : Intent]**, using **[Reality Notes : Chronicle]** and **[Existing Anchors : Targets]** as constraints.

**Optional blanks (descriptions)**

* **[Reality Notes : Chronicle]** — time/scope/constraints that force the slice to be small.
* **[Existing Anchors : Targets]** — surfaces/contracts you must stay compatible with.

---

### What to Produce

Produce two small TEXT packs:

**A) Focus Intent Pack (the runnable slice)**
Must be achievable in **1–3 downstream actions**.

Include:

1. **Focus Aim** — one sentence: the “one thing” to make true.
2. **Focus Subject / Surface** — the concrete thing being changed/understood/defined.
3. **Focus Constraints** — 1–3 constraints that keep it small.
4. **Focus Done Signal** — observable success.

**B) Deferred Pack (what is intentionally not done now)**
Include:
5) **Deferred Items** — 2–6 short lines naming what was cut (no plan, no steps).
6) **Return Condition (optional)** — one sentence describing when it’s worth revisiting deferred items.

Hard guard:

* Do not invent a new goal; preserve the user’s intent but slice it.
* Do not include implementation steps or patch plans.
* Do not ask questions.

---

### Recommended Chunk Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Focus Aim** — the single now-true outcome for this slice.
* **Focus Subject / Surface** — what the slice is about, in concrete nouns.
* **Focus Constraints** — the limits that keep it winnable.
* **Focus Done Signal** — observable proof the slice is complete.
* **Deferred Items** — what is explicitly not being done now (names only).
* **Return Condition (optional)** — when it makes sense to pick up the deferred items.
