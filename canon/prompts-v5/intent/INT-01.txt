## INT-01 — Intent Transformer (Clarify)

### Input Grammar

**Simple prose (2 required blanks)**
Clarify **[Raw Ask : Intent]** into a clean intent for **[Intended Use : Intent]**.

**Required blanks (2)**

* **[Raw Ask : Intent]** — the user’s messy message (what they typed).
* **[Intended Use : Intent]** — what this intent will drive next (e.g., “run FI-02 fix”, “plan FP-02 done receipt”, “generate a patch segment”).

**Pro prose (optional boosters)**
Clarify **[Raw Ask : Intent]** into a clean intent for **[Intended Use : Intent]**, anchored by **[Existing Anchors : Targets]** and constrained by **[Reality Notes : Chronicle]**.

**Optional blanks (descriptions)**

* **[Existing Anchors : Targets]** — prior artifact packs that must anchor meaning (surface/contract/plan already chosen).
* **[Reality Notes : Chronicle]** — constraints/observations that must be respected (limits, known behavior, scope pressure).

---

### What to Produce

Produce an **Intent Pack** as a small set of TEXT chunks that is:

* coherent (no contradictions),
* specific enough to drive **1–3 downstream actions**, and
* free of implementation/solution details (no patch plans, no code).

Across the pack, include:

1. **Core Aim** — one sentence stating what should become true.
2. **Subject / Surface** — concrete nouns for what the aim is about (feature/slice/surface).
3. **Constraints** — 1–3 non-negotiables (limits, preserve-true, scope fences).
4. **Done Signal** — what counts as success (observable outcome, not vibes).
5. **Non-Goals (optional)** — what not to do / not expand into.
6. **Assumptions (optional)** — only if required to make the intent runnable; write as **“Assumed: …”** (no questions).

---

### Recommended Chunk Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Aim (Now-True)** — the single outcome you’re trying to make true.
* **Subject / Surface** — the specific thing this intent is about, named in concrete nouns.
* **Constraints** — the hard boundaries and “must stay true” conditions.
* **Done Signal** — the observable proof that the intent succeeded.
* **Non-Goals (optional)** — explicit exclusions that prevent scope creep.
* **Assumptions (optional)** — any necessary assumptions stated firmly as “Assumed: …”.
