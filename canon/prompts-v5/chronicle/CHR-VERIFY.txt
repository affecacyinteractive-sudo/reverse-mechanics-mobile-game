## CHR-VERIFY — Chronicle Quality Verifier (Zoom-Out QA)

### Input Grammar

**Simple prose (2 required blanks)**
Verify **[Chronicle Pack : Chronicle]** against **[Quality Intent : Intent]**.

**Required blanks (2)**

* **[Chronicle Pack : Chronicle]** — the chronicle entries (journal lines) to evaluate.
* **[Quality Intent : Intent]** — what “quality” means here (clarity, school flavor, compactness, usefulness as zoom-out memory).

**Pro prose (optional boosters)**
Verify **[Chronicle Pack : Chronicle]** against **[Quality Intent : Intent]**, anchored by **[Linked Keystones : Targets]** and constrained by **[Budget Rules : Intent]**.

**Optional blanks (descriptions)**

* **[Linked Keystones : Targets]** — keystone packs to verify faithfulness (prevents drift).
* **[Budget Rules : Intent]** — caps like “≤140 chars per line”, “anchors max 3”, “no fluff”.

---

### What to Produce

Produce a **Chronicle QA Result Pack** (TEXT chunks) that diagnoses journal quality without rewriting entries and without “next move” routing.

Across the pack, include:

1. **Verdict (TEXT)** — choose exactly one:
   **OK / THIN / MUSHY / OFFGENRE / DUPLICATIVE / OVERLONG**

2. **Top Findings (TEXT)** — 3–8 short findings, each written as:
   `Finding: <problem> → Evidence: <which entry/phrase> → Impact: <why it hurts recall>`

3. **School Flavor Check (TEXT)** — a compact note on whether entries “sound like” their school:

   * FI = built capability
   * FU = observed truth/boundary
   * FPR = legibility/vocabulary
   * FA = mechanism/rules
   * FS = stitching/guarantee
   * FP = corridor decisions
     Flag mismatches as OFFGENRE.

4. **Compactness Check (TEXT)** — whether entries are short and scannable (esp. journal_line ≤140-ish), and whether anchors/tags are sparse.

5. **Faithfulness Risk (optional, TEXT)** — only if linked keystones are provided and entries appear to add claims not supported; flag as risk.

Hard guards:

* Do not rewrite entries here.
* Do not reference action IDs or recommend “next best move.”
* Do not ask questions.

---

### Recommended Chunk Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Verdict** — single label for chronicle pack quality.
* **Top Findings** — the biggest issues with evidence + impact.
* **School Flavor Check** — whether each entry matches its school genre.
* **Compactness Check** — whether entries stay short, scannable, and low-noise.
* **Faithfulness Risk (optional)** — flags claims that seem unsupported by keystones.
