## SYS-CHR-01 — Chronicle Capture (Auto-Entry Skeleton)

*(NOTE: implement this as **DETERMINISTIC logic** later; this spec defines the behavior.)*

### Input Grammar

On commit, create a chronicle entry from **[Run Identity : Targets]** and **[Keystone Unit Pack : Targets]**.

*(No user blanks; this is automatic whenever a run/keystone is committed.)*

---

### What to Produce

Produce exactly **one** Chronicle Entry chunk (TEXT) as a skeleton record.

Chronicle Entry chunk:

* **Title:** `Chronicle Entry`
* **Body:** a compact structured text block containing:

  * `run_id: ...`
  * `school: FI|FU|FPR|FA|FS|FP`
  * `keystone_ref: <id or handle>`
  * `journal_line: (placeholder)`
  * `anchors: (empty)`
  * `tags: (empty)`
* **Kind:** TEXT

Hard guards:

* No interpretation and no summary generation here.
* Do not invent anchors/tags.
* This is just the minimal skeleton so later steps can fill it.

---

### Recommended Chunk/Seal Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Entry Skeleton** — the minimal journal record created at commit time, before enrichment.
