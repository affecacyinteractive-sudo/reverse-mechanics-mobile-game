## CHR-02 — Chronicle Rewriter (Tone + Clarity, School-Biased)

### Input Grammar

**Simple prose (2 required blanks)**
Rewrite **[Chronicle Entries : Chronicle]** for **[Rewrite Intent : Intent]**.

**Required blanks (2)**

* **[Chronicle Entries : Chronicle]** — the selected journal entries to improve (one or more).
* **[Rewrite Intent : Intent]** — what “better” means (clearer, tighter, more concrete, more school-flavored).

**Pro prose (optional boosters)**
Rewrite **[Chronicle Entries : Chronicle]** for **[Rewrite Intent : Intent]**, constrained by **[Style Constraints : Intent]** and anchored by **[Linked Keystone Packs : Targets]**.

**Optional blanks (descriptions)**

* **[Style Constraints : Intent]** — caps and tone (“≤140 chars each”, “keep assertive”, “avoid hype”, “no jargon”).
* **[Linked Keystone Packs : Targets]** — keystone content that the entries should remain faithful to (prevents drift).

---

### What to Produce

Produce a **Rewritten Chronicle Pack** (TEXT chunks) that preserves meaning but improves readability and school-specific emphasis.

For each input chronicle entry, output:

1. **Rewritten Journal Line (TEXT)** — ≤140 chars preferred; one sentence max.
2. **Preserved Anchors (TEXT, optional)** — 1–3 anchors/handles, only if present in input or in linked keystone packs.
3. **School Flavor Rule (implicit)** — the rewrite must emphasize what the school cares about:

   * FI: “now-true capability + seam”
   * FU: “observed truth/boundary”
   * FPR: “how to navigate/name”
   * FA: “mechanism/rule/invariant”
   * FS: “stitch/instantiate + guarantee”
   * FP: “corridor decision (goal/fence/tripwire)”

Hard guards:

* Do not change the factual content (no new claims).
* Do not add implementation detail or code.
* No action IDs and no “next move” routing.

---

### Recommended Chunk Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Before → After Lines** — rewritten journal lines that preserve meaning and become more legible.
* **Anchor Preservation (optional)** — keep or tighten the anchors/handles without inventing new ones.
* **School Emphasis** — each line should “sound like” its school genre while staying short.
