## CHR-PROG-REPAIR — Journey Progress Report Repair (Minimal)

### Input Grammar

**Simple prose (2 required blanks)**
Repair **[Journey Progress Pack : Targets]** using **[Aggregate Plan Pack : Targets]**.

**Required blanks (2)**

* **[Journey Progress Pack : Targets]** — the output of CHR-PROG-01 (may be thin, inconsistent, or poorly mapped).
* **[Aggregate Plan Pack : Targets]** — the plan spine the report must reference accurately.

**Pro prose (optional boosters)**
Repair **[Journey Progress Pack : Targets]** using **[Aggregate Plan Pack : Targets]**, grounded by **[Chronicle Window : Chronicle]** and guided by **[Verifier Notes : Chronicle]**.

**Optional blanks (descriptions)**

* **[Chronicle Window : Chronicle]** — the journal evidence the report must map to (prevents overclaims).
* **[Verifier Notes : Chronicle]** — notes about what was wrong (missing mapping, unclear verdict, too verbose).

---

### What to Produce

Produce a **Repaired Journey Progress Pack** (TEXT chunks) that is:

* faithful to the aggregate plan structure,
* faithful to the provided chronicle evidence (if provided),
* compact and internally consistent.

Minimal repair operations allowed:

1. **Fix mapping gaps** — ensure each pillar has at least one clear reflection line.
2. **Remove overclaims** — downgrade claims lacking journal evidence to “unclear / not signaled here.”
3. **Tighten evidence references** — replace vague evidence with short anchor/entry-fragment references, or remove if unsupported.
4. **Compress verbosity** — reduce long sections into short lines while preserving meaning.
5. **Normalize verdict coherence** — overall verdict must match pillar reflections (no contradictions).

Hard guards:

* Do not propose next moves or new plans.
* Do not invent journal evidence.
* No action IDs and no “next move” routing.

---

### Recommended Chunk Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Verdict Coherence** — overall verdict aligns with pillar reflections.
* **Pillar Coverage** — each plan pillar has a clear, compact reflection.
* **Evidence Hygiene** — evidence references are grounded or removed.
* **Gap Clarity** — unclear areas stated plainly without speculation.
* **Compression** — report stays readable and small.
