## CHR-NORM — Chronicle Normalizer (Format + Legibility)

### Input Grammar

**Simple prose (2 required blanks)**
Normalize **[Chronicle Pack : Chronicle]** for **[Normalization Intent : Intent]**.

**Required blanks (2)**

* **[Chronicle Pack : Chronicle]** — the chronicle entries to rewrite for clarity and consistency.
* **[Normalization Intent : Intent]** — what to improve (shorten, remove fluff, standardize tense, make school flavor clearer).

**Pro prose (optional boosters)**
Normalize **[Chronicle Pack : Chronicle]** for **[Normalization Intent : Intent]**, guided by **[Verifier Notes : Chronicle]** and constrained by **[Budget Rules : Intent]**.

**Optional blanks (descriptions)**

* **[Verifier Notes : Chronicle]** — CHR-VERIFY findings (overlong, mushy, offgenre, duplicative).
* **[Budget Rules : Intent]** — caps like “≤140 chars”, “one sentence”, “anchors max 3”.

---

### What to Produce

Produce a **Normalized Chronicle Pack** (TEXT chunks) that preserves meaning but improves legibility and consistency.

Allowed edits (format-only):

1. **Length cap** — compress each journal line to one sentence (≤140 chars preferred).
2. **Remove fluff** — delete filler words and vague praise; keep concrete nouns and verbs.
3. **School flavor clarity** — rewrite wording so each line better reflects its school genre *without adding new facts*.
4. **Standardize tense** — prefer past-tense “We built / We verified / We named / We extracted / We stitched / We decided” style.
5. **Anchor hygiene** — keep 0–3 anchors; remove noisy/duplicative anchors; do not invent new anchors.

Hard guards:

* Do not add new claims not present in the original entries/linked keystones.
* Do not add implementation detail or code.
* No action IDs and no “next move” routing.

---

### Recommended Chunk Beat Categories (with 1-line descriptions)

* **Line Compression** — each entry rewritten to be short and scannable.
* **Fluff Removal** — vague language removed, concrete nouns/verbs retained.
* **Genre Alignment** — wording adjusted to better match school flavor.
* **Tense Consistency** — entries standardized to a consistent journal voice.
* **Anchor Hygiene** — anchors trimmed to a sparse, high-signal set.
