INPUT GRAMMAR *(User Journey Story / what it feels like)*
### Pro prose (same core + action-specific INPUT blanks)
Tell the story of using **[Subject / Slice : Targets]** toward **[Journey Intent : Intent]**, grounded by **[Touchpoint Evidence : Packets]** and validated by **[User Feel Notes : Chronicle]**.

**Required INPUT blanks (4)**
- **[Subject / Slice : Targets]** — the feature/flow the user is experiencing.
- **[Journey Intent : Intent]** — what the story should clarify (onboard, sell value, reveal friction, document behavior).
- **[Touchpoint Evidence : Packets]** — snippets that reveal real entry points/events/surfaces the journey should mention.
- **[User Feel Notes : Chronicle]** — what users try, where they stumble, what “success” feels like, and what hurts.


WHAT TO PRODUCE (contract)
Produce one tight user-journey story:
1) Audience: 1 phrase naming who this journey is for (if implied, you may omit).
2) Entry Context: 1–2 sentences describing where the user starts (what they have/see/assume).
3) Step Path: 3–6 beats written as “user action → system response.”
4) Outcome Receipt: 1 sentence describing the visible end state.
5) Boundary Moment: 1 beat describing a bend (stumble/limit/edge).
6) Boundary Moment Type: one word (common stumble / rare edge).
7) Continuation Hook (optional): 1 sentence describing what naturally comes next.
8) Closure Check: 1 sentence stating what the reader should now understand/predict.
